Are dental implants safe for diabetics? ▼
Yes, dental implants can be safe for patients with diabetes if the condition is well controlled. Consultation with the dentist is essential before treatment.
How long does the implant procedure take? ▼
The procedure usually takes 1–2 hours per implant, followed by a healing period before the crown is placed.
Will the implants look natural? ▼
Absolutely! Dental implants are designed to look and function just like your natural teeth.
